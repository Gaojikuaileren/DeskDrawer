Frequently asked questions

Is DeskDrawer a one-time purchase?
Yes. DeskDrawer is a one-time purchase — buy it once on the Microsoft Store and use it for life. There is no subscription, no renewal, and no in-app purchases.
Does it move or change my files?
No. Your files stay in their original desktop location. DeskDrawer only remembers which drawer each icon, folder, or shortcut belongs to. If you uninstall it, your files remain untouched.
Does it work on Windows 11 and Windows 10?
Yes. DeskDrawer runs on Windows 10 and Windows 11 (version 10.0.19041 or newer), and uses the native Windows desktop icon operations you already know.
Does it collect my data or show ads?
No. DeskDrawer has no ads, no telemetry, no analytics, and no accounts. It runs entirely on your device and makes no network connections of its own.
